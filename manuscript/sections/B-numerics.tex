\section{Appendix: numerical verification record}\label{app:numerics}

All scripts live in the program repository; this appendix records what
was verified and at what scale. Machine verification is treated as
evidence about \emph{truth}, never as proof; statements verified here
but not proved carry their own status tags in the text.

\subsection*{Lemma 0 (\texttt{lemma0\_test.py})}
Both sandwich inclusions: zero exceptions on $3\le n\le6\times10^4$.
$|D_0^{(+)}|=|D_0|$ exactly on this range; $|D_0^{(-)}|$ is $2\%$
larger; the lower set captures $98.7\%$ of $\W$. Margin symmetric
difference measured $\approx6\times10^{-5}$ at $10^9$. Measured pair
density at $10^9$: $0.92\,c_1^2$ (finite-scale, converging, drift
direction matching FK's own slow convergence).

\subsection*{Deep large sieve (\texttt{verify\_dls.py})}
The homomorphism identities \eqref{eq:fq-hom}, Wieferich orthogonality,
and the linearization of Lemma~\ref{lem:linearization}: exact (randomized,
$q\le1009$, and re-run June 9, 2026 on the program machine: all PASS).
Energy bound of Proposition~\ref{prop:energy}: ratio
$E/(N^2/q+qN)\le0.75$ across $q\in\{101,401,1009\}$, $N\le3q^2$,
approaching $1$ at $N\asymp q^2$ as the proof predicts. Observed RMS of
$|S(\lambda)|$ over initial segments far below the proven $\sqrt{qN}$
(e.g.\ $q=1009$, $N=255272$: observed $356$ vs guaranteed $16049$ vs
trivial $255272$).

\subsection*{Two-frequency reduction at $x_{\mathrm{model}}=10^{11}$
(\texttt{wp21\_test.py})}

$\sigma\equiv1$: linearized phase equals the true Fermat-quotient phase
to $10^{-14}$; median $z=|V|/\sqrt{R/4}=0.08$, 90th percentile $0.40$,
aggregate $\sum_p|V_p|=0.8\%$ of trivial; major arcs observed exactly on
the predicted curves ($p=4759,4813\approx q$: the $\mu/\lambda=1$ curve,
$|V|\approx0.3R$, $z\approx41$; $p=8171$ at $\lambda=2$:
$(p/q)^2\approx2.91$, the $\mu/\lambda=3$ curve). All 26 cases exceeding
a naively truncated bound trace to major-arc width undercounting, none
to a failure of the mechanism.
